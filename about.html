Our Story

A Passion for the Finer Things

Cuban Cigar World is Australia's premier destination for cigar enthusiasts and lovers of luxury. We've built our reputation on an unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity, and the art of fine living.

How It Started

From Humble Beginnings

What started as a passion project among friends who shared a love for fine cigars has grown into Australia's most trusted online cigar destination. Founded in Melbourne in 2014, Cuban Cigar World was born from a simple desire: to bring the world's finest cigars and luxury goods to Australian enthusiasts.

Over the years, we've expanded our collection to include not just premium cigars from Cuba, the Dominican Republic, and Nicaragua, but also carefully curated luxury gifts, handcrafted accessories, and unique collectibles that embody the spirit of refined living.

Today, we serve thousands of satisfied customers across Australia, each one trusting us to deliver authentic products, exceptional service, and an experience worthy of the finest things in life.
